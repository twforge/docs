= Planner

Calculate timing for coordinated attacks.

== Time Calculator

Figure out when to send attacks:

. Go to **Planner**
. Enter your **source village**
. Enter the **target village**
. Select the **slowest unit** in your attack
. Enter the desired **arrival time**
. Click **Calculate**

The Planner shows exactly when to send your attack.

== Multiple Waves

To coordinate several attacks landing together:

. Add multiple source villages
. Set the same arrival time for all
. The Planner calculates each send time
. Send each attack at its calculated time

== Travel Time Reference

Quickly check travel times:

. Enter source and target
. Select a unit type
. See the travel duration instantly

Use this to estimate how far in advance to plan.

== Tips

* Account for your reaction time when planning
* Double-check server time vs. your local time
* For large operations, give yourself buffer time
* Coordinate with tribe members on exact timing
