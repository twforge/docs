= Reports

Manage and search your battle reports.

== Importing Reports

=== Automatic Import (Recommended)

With the userscript installed:

. Play the game normally
. View any battle report in-game
. The report is automatically saved to TW Forge

=== Manual Import

. Copy a battle report from the game
. Go to **Reports** â†’ **Import**
. Paste the report
. Click **Import**

== Browsing Reports

All your imported reports appear in the list.

* **Sort** by date, type, or result
* **Filter** by attack/defense/support
* **Search** by player or village name

== Report Details

Click any report to see:

* Full battle details
* Units sent and lost
* Resources captured
* Building damage
* Loyalty changes

== Filtering Reports

Use filters to find specific reports:

. Click **Filters**
. Set your criteria:
** Date range
** Report type (attack, defense, support, scout)
** Outcome (win, loss)
** Player or village
. Click **Apply**

== Exporting Data

To export your reports:

. Apply filters to select reports
. Click **Export**
. Choose format (CSV, etc.)
. Download the file

== Tips

* Install the userscript for automatic import
* Use date filters to focus on recent operations
* Check reports before planning new attacks
