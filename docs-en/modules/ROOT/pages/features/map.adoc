= Map

The interactive map shows all villages in your world.

== Viewing the Map

. Go to **Map** from the menu
. The map loads showing your area
. Use your mouse to navigate:
** **Scroll** to zoom in/out
** **Drag** to move around
** **Click** a village for details

== Finding Villages

To locate a specific village:

. Use the search bar at the top
. Enter a village name, coordinates, or player name
. Click the result to jump to that location

TIP: Enter coordinates like `500|500` to jump directly to that spot.

== Understanding Colors

Villages are colored by ownership:

* **Your villages** – Highlighted distinctly
* **Tribe members** – Same color as your tribe
* **Enemy tribes** – Different colors per tribe
* **Barbarian villages** – Neutral color

== Using Overlays

Overlays add extra information to the map:

. Click the **Layers** button
. Toggle overlays on/off:
** Tribe territories
** Continent borders
** Custom markers

== Marking Targets

To mark villages for operations:

. Click a village
. Click **Mark as Target**
. The village shows a marker on the map
. Use these markers when planning in Action Planner

== Tips

* Zoom out to see the big picture
* Use tribe coloring to spot enemy clusters
* Combine with Action Planner for attack visualization
